      How to use

      👥 Groups

      Tap New Group on the home screen to create a group. Give it a name, organisation, email address, icon and colour.

      ✅ Taking attendance

      Tap a group to open it. Tap each name to mark them present. Use Select all or Clear all to set the whole list at once.

      📧 Sending the register

      Tap Send Email at the bottom. Your mail app opens with the subject and body pre-filled. Attendance resets automatically after 1.5 seconds.

      💾 Backing up your data

      Your data is stored on this device only. Go to Settings and tap Backup Data. Save to iCloud Drive after every session.
