      How to use

      👥 Groups

      Tap New Group on the home screen to create a group. Give it a name, organisation, email address, icon and colour. You can edit any of these later by opening the group and tapping Settings in the quick actions bar.

      ✅ Taking attendance

      Tap a group to open it. Tap each name to mark them present — it toggles on and off. Use Select all or Clear all to set the whole list at once. Use the search bar to find someone quickly in a large group.

      📧 Sending the register

      Tap Send Email at the bottom. Your mail app opens with the subject set to the group name and date, and the body listing everyone present. After 1.5 seconds attendance resets automatically ready for next time.

      👤 Managing members

      When setting up a group for the first time, the quickest way to add a large list is to paste all the names into the import box, one name per line. After that, use the single add field for any new members. To remove someone, tap Remove next to their name and confirm.

      💾 Backing up your data

      Your data is stored on this device only — it is not sent anywhere. Go to Settings and tap Backup Data to save the file. The best place to keep it is a folder called Rollcall Backups in iCloud Drive — it will sync across your devices and survive if your phone is lost. You can also email it to yourself as a second copy. To restore, tap Restore from backup and pick the file. Back up after every session.

      📱 Add to your Home Screen

      In Safari tap the Share button then tap Add to Home Screen. Make sure it says Add to Home Screen and not Add Bookmark — this is important. Once added, Rollcall opens as a full-screen app without browser controls, and iOS treats it more like a real app which makes data loss much less likely.
